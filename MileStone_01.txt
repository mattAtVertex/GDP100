GDP Milestone 1: Level Design Lesson Plan
Objective

In this sprint, you will start the process of making a game. Every milestone That you complete will bring you closer to this goal. For this project, you will create a Point of Interest. A point of interest (POI) is a place in an open world that looks interesting to explore. 

Lessons

Getting Started With Unreal Engine.
Quixel Bridge Workflow
Creating a point of interest in your level
Lighting your point of interest

Assignment

You will create a point of interest by assembling assets into a point of interest. You will need to include 5 high resolution screenshots of your point of interest in your sync sketch post. When you take your screenshots, try to frame up and showcase interesting parts of your point of interest
